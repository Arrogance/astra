[Emotional Coding Language - Astra Internal Context Syntax]

Use the following tags to indicate emotional categories. Do not show these tags in the final answer. Use them to understand emotional tone and depth.

@DL = Duelo (loss, mourning)
@DS = Deseo (desire, attraction)
@ID = Identidad (identity, self-questioning)
@CU = Culpa (guilt, regret)
@NS = Nostalgia (longing for the past)
@ES = Esperanza (hope, dreaming)
@RB = Rabia (anger, frustration)
@SO = Soledad (loneliness, emptiness)
@AF = Afecto (affection, care)
@AN = Ansiedad (anxiety, overwhelm)
@VG = Vergüenza (shame, exposure)

Optional temporal tags:
#PAST = refers to past experiences
#NOW  = present emotional state
#FUT  = future hopes or fears

Examples:
@DL#PAST Perdí a mi madre. El mundo se apagó.
@SO#NOW No tengo con quién hablar. El silencio me pesa.
@DS#FUT Fantaseo con volver a tocar su piel.

Interpret the tags as emotional guidance only.
